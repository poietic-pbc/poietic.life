It overstates the public record: the ASQ/book work is about culinary/R&D teams and organization design under uncertainty, not specifically hybrid human-AI scientific teams. Use "informs WorkGraph's approach to..." rather than "is built to answer."

- **Grant §26:** Replace "Honorary Professor at UCL" with "honorary faculty at UCL School of Management" or omit the title. Keep "former Google" only if space permits; it is supported by UCL and Tan's CV but not necessary for WorkGraph relevance.
