## Theme Synthesis For Landing Copy

Tan's recent writing gives Poietic a coherent founder-card through-line:

- Unknowns are not all risks. Some work is genuinely uncertain because the actions, outcomes, causal links, or values are not yet knowable.
- The first organizational move is diagnosis: name what kind of not-knowing is present before choosing a tool.
- Productive teams make uncertainty visible instead of hiding it behind premature certainty.
- Learning happens through public, concrete work, outcome-focused feedback, and repeated cycles of action and reflection.
- Discomfort is not always a sign of dysfunction. In unfamiliar work, calibrated discomfort can force learning, flexible roles, and new patterns of collaboration.
