01/04 mission
The next breakthrough in AI for science is not better models.
It is better organization.

AI agents can now search literature, write code, analyze data, and design molecules. But complex scientific problems require teams of agents and humans coordinating across days or weeks. No existing tool provides this coordination.

Poietic builds open-source infrastructure that makes hybrid human–AI research auditable, reproducible, and inspectable. Our first product is WorkGraph — a task coordination graph where both humans and AI agents claim, execute, and complete interdependent work, with the full process recorded in a browsable computation graph.
